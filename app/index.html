About BloodGroupDetector

Our Mission

BloodDetect aims to revolutionize how blood group testing is performed by eliminating the need for invasive blood draws. Our technology leverages cutting-edge machine learning algorithms to analyze fingerprint patterns and correlate them with blood group characteristics.

Founded in 2023, our team of biomedical engineers, data scientists, and healthcare professionals work tirelessly to improve the accuracy and accessibility of blood group testing worldwide.

The Science Behind It

Research has shown correlations between fingerprint patterns and various genetic traits, including blood groups. Our AI model has been trained on thousands of fingerprint images with known blood groups to identify these subtle correlations.

While our technology is not intended to replace traditional blood tests for medical procedures, it provides a convenient, preliminary screening method that can be particularly useful in emergencies or remote locations.

Accuracy & Reliability

95% Accuracy

Our current model achieves 95% accuracy in controlled testing environments. We continuously improve our algorithms with new data to increase reliability.

Privacy Commitment

We take your privacy seriously. All fingerprint images are anonymized and encrypted. We never share your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent.
